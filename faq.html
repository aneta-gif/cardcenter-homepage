Can I use a discounted card anywhere the brand is accepted?

Yes, unless a specific restriction is noted on that brand's page — we call those out clearly next to the price, never buried in fine print.

Selling

How do you decide my offer?

Your offer is based on the brand's resale demand and the remaining balance — shown instantly in the quote widget, before you send anything.

How long does payout take?

Usually 1–2 business days after we verify the balance, straight to your bank account or PayPal. No minimum payout.

Can a payout be reversed after I've already been paid?

No. Once we verify a card and pay you for it, that transaction is closed — we don't claw back a payout weeks later because a buyer had an issue.

Verification & Trust

Why do you need my ID?

Identity verification protects both buyers and sellers on real financial transactions like these — the same kind of check a bank runs before it trusts a new payee.

What do you do with my information?

We use it only to confirm you're you, then store it securely. We never sell it.

How long does verification take?

About 90 seconds for most people. If it ever takes longer, we'll tell you honestly instead of leaving the screen frozen on "checking."

Guarantee

What does CardCenter Shield actually cover?

If a card's balance doesn't match what we told you, you get a full refund within 100 days — no partial credit, no back-and-forth.

What if I don't use the card right away?

Coverage lasts 100 days from purchase, so you have real time to actually use it, not just a same-day check.

How do I make a claim?

From your wallet, open the card and select "Report an issue." You'll see a live status the whole time — submitted, reviewing, resolved — never a bare "pending" with no update.

Payments

What payment methods do you accept?

Major debit and credit cards for purchases. Sell-side payouts go to a bank account or PayPal.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes — payments are processed under PCI DSS standards, and we never store your full card number on our servers.

Will I get a receipt?

Yes, emailed immediately after purchase, billed by CardCenter LLC — the same name on your card and your statement, never a second unexplained entity.
